# AnglersOS 🎣
**The ultimate open-source ecosystem for smart fishing.**

AnglersOS is a comprehensive, open-source hardware and software suite designed for professional carp and bank fishing. It moves beyond simple bite alarms, creating a unified ecosystem of sensors, security devices, and smart lighting.

{{% notice info %}}
This project is currently in active development. Follow our [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@AnglersOS) for the latest updates.
{{% /notice %}}

### ✨ Key Features

#### 🎣 Smart Bite Alarms
*   **High Precision:** Advanced 3D magnetic sensing for micro-bite detection.
*   **Intelligent Filtering:** Software-based wave and debris filtering to eliminate false alarms.
*   **Ultra-Low Power:** Optimized for months of standby on a single rechargeable cell.
*   **Anti-Theft:** Built-in motion sensors to trigger alarms if the rod or pod is moved.

#### 🛡️ Security & Lighting
*   **360° Surveillance:** Smart lanterns with multiple motion sensors for full camp perimeter monitoring.
*   **Smart Automation:** "Friend or Foe" detection—lanterns light up when you approach and secure the camp when you leave.
*   **Power Bank Function:** Lanterns act as a high-capacity power source for your other devices.

#### 📟 Connectivity & UI
*   **Long Range:** Rock-solid wireless connection even in thick brush or forest.
*   **Central Pager:** Dedicated gateway device with a high-resolution round display.
*   **Mobile App:** Cross-platform control via Bluetooth (Android and iOS).
